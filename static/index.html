Why EV road trips are harder

Planning an EV trip requires tracking too many variables. Existing navigation apps leave you stressed instead of excited.

Battery Anxiety

Drivers constantly worry about running out of battery before the next station.

Charging Uncertainty

Finding reliable, fast charging stations along your specific route is a puzzle.

Poor Break Planning

Drivers struggle to time rest stops perfectly with charging sessions.

Missed Experiences

Navigation tools prioritize speed over discovering interesting places along the way.
